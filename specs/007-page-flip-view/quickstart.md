# Quickstart: Page Flip Animation View

## Setup
1. Ensure you have at least 2-3 diary entries saved in your account.
2. Run `npm run dev`.

## Verification Steps

### 1. Entering Book Mode
- Go to `/dashboard`.
- Click the "Read as Book" (책 모드로 읽기) button.
- **Verify**: A full-screen overlay opens showing an open notebook.

### 2. Flipping Pages
- Click the right-bottom corner of the book.
- **Verify**: The page flips smoothly to the next entry.
- Try dragging the page edge manually.
- **Verify**: The paper curves and follows the cursor.

### 3. Navigation Controls
- Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons at the bottom of the overlay.
- **Verify**: The book flips to the corresponding pages.

### 4. Exiting
- Click the "Close" (X) button in the top corner.
- **Verify**: Returned to the Dashboard with your previous filters intact.
